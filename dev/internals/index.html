Ferrite.reinit! — Function
Ferrite.reinit!(c::CellBuffer, dh::AbstractDofHandler, cellnum::Int, anew, aold)

Reinitialize the c::CellBuffer for cell number cellnum. The global degree of freedom vectors anew (current) and aold are used to update the cell degree of freedom vectors in c. If the global vectors are instead ::Nothing, the corresponding cell values are set to NaN The element stiffness, c.Ke, and residual, c.re, are also zeroed.
